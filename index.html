IEEE Signal Processing Cup 2026

This project implements a high performance audio processing pipeline designed for the IEEE Signal Processing Cup 2026, targeting the extraction of a male speaker from complex mixtures with high female interference. It utilizes a hybrid architecture combining classical spatial filtering such as MVDR beamforming and Griffiths-Jim adaptive cancellation with a deep learning-based CleanUNet model for refined denoising. The system is optimized for both anechoic and reverberant environments, featuring specialized sub steps for early leakage cleanup and local artifact de-clicking to ensure superior speech clarity.

Machine Learning and IoT Based Automated Remote Health Monitoring and Alert System

Publication Date: 7 May 2026
Conference: IEEE RAAICON 2025
Publisher: IEEE

This paper presents the design and implementation of a low-cost, portable, Machine Learning and IoT-based remote patient health monitoring system integrated with an emergency alert mechanism. The system continuously monitors critical health parameters including heart rate, blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂), body temperature, and ECG signals, while also incorporating fall detection and toxic gas monitoring for enhanced patient safety. An intelligent diagnostic framework was developed using a 1D CNN model in Google Colab to automatically analyze ECG data and detect abnormalities, achieving an accuracy of approximately 94.23%. Powered by an ESP32 microcontroller and multiple biomedical sensors, the system enables real-time monitoring through onboard OLED and TFT displays as well as a mobile application dashboard. In emergency situations, instant alerts are transmitted to caregivers via a Telegram bot for rapid response. Experimental evaluations demonstrated an overall measurement accuracy of nearly 97%, while maintaining a highly cost-effective design with a total implementation cost of only $25.45 USD, making it a practical alternative to many commercially available smart health monitoring devices.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1109/RAAICON69033.2025.11502033
